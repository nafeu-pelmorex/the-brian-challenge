The Brian Challenge

Rules

  1. Last person to arrive after 9am gets 1 point
  2. If you are the only person after 9am, you get 1 point
  3. Points apply only on a week to week basis
  4. Person who loses has to treat other competitors to a customized Starbucks beverage at a time most convenient for them
  5. Excuses must be approved by Brian
  6. Brian can change any and all rules of the game at any time without notice (and retroactively)
  7. Any competitor who works from home must take a selfie at their desk with their work open before 9am
  8. Any competitor who leaves the challenge midway MUST commit to an exit punishment of buying everyone customized Starbucks beverages
  9. If there is a tie between all competitors, the last one to come in the next day is the loser regardless of when they come in (no time restrictions)
